## Overview

Health Box is an end-to-end healthcare management system built for clinics and hospitals. It simplifies:

- Patient registration & digital medical records
- Appointment scheduling and rescheduling
- Prescription generation and printing
- Billing and invoice management
- Doctor dashboards
- Role-based access control
- Inventory and medicine tracking
- Analytics and reports

---

## Features

### Authentication & Roles
- JWT authentication
- Role-based access:
  - Admin
  - Doctor
  - Receptionist
  - Patient
- Secure password encryption

### Patient Management
- Create patient profiles
- Medical history tracking
- Visit records
- EMR support

### Appointment Management
- Online booking
- Rescheduling
- Cancellation
- Queue management

### Prescription System
- Digital prescriptions
- PDF generation
- Patient medication history

### Billing
- Invoice generation
- Payment tracking
- Revenue reports

### Inventory
- Medicine stock
- Alerts for low inventory
- Supplier tracking

### Dashboard
- Daily appointments
- Revenue overview
- Active patients
- Pending bills
